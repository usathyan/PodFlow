PodFlow is a modern, beautifully designed podcast player that puts your listening experience first. Built with the latest Android technologies for a smooth, responsive experience.

BEAUTIFUL DESIGN
• Stunning tiled home screen with your podcast artwork
• Material You dynamic colors that match your wallpaper
• Smooth animations and intuitive gestures
• Dark mode and AMOLED black themes

POWERFUL PLAYBACK
• Variable playback speed (0.5x to 3x)
• Smart silence trimming to save time
• Volume boost for quiet podcasts
• Per-podcast playback settings
• Sleep timer with gentle fade-out
• Chapter support with thumbnails

SMART QUEUE MANAGEMENT
• Drag and drop to reorder episodes
• Swipe to remove from queue
• Auto-queue new episodes
• Smart episode filters

OFFLINE LISTENING
• Download episodes for offline playback
• WiFi-only download option
• Automatic cleanup of played episodes
• Storage management tools

DISCOVER NEW SHOWS
• Browse trending podcasts
• Search by category
• iTunes and Podcast Index integration
• OPML import/export

SYNC & BACKUP
• gpodder.net synchronization
• Local backup and restore
• Cross-device sync

PRIVACY FOCUSED
• No ads, no tracking
• No account required
• Your data stays on your device
• Open source (GPL v3)

OPEN SOURCE
PodFlow is built on the foundation of AntennaPod, one of the most trusted open-source podcast apps. We're committed to keeping podcasting open and accessible to everyone.

Source code: github.com/AntennaPod/AntennaPod
